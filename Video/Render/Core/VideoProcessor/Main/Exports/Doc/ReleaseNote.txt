VideoProcessor.dll Release Note

Ver. 0.0.0.1
Release Date 2008/08/15
First version: Implement software video processor:
1. Support median, bob and average de-interlacer.
2. Support NV12->YUY2, NV12->NV12, NV12->YV12 and YV12->YUY2 color space converter.
3. Support video color effect.
4. Optimize render performance with MMX, SSE, and SSE2 Intrinsics.
